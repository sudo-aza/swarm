\vspace{2cm}
{\bfseries\color{blue} Setup:} Fill page to ~5cm from bottom, then place a tall figure
(6cm) that will be deferred. The wrapped paragraph is 1 lipsum (~6 lines).
After the paragraph ends, regular (non-wrapped) text follows.

\vspace{4cm}

\begin{swarmwrap}%

    \centering
    \color{red}\rule{\linewidth}{5.5cm}
    \captionof{figure}{Test 1: Tall deferred figure (5.5cm).}
  \end{minipage}%
\end{swarmwrap}%
\swarmwrapnext
\lipsum[1]

this text could overlap the bottom of the red figure on the next page.
Check the next page for overlap between this green text and the red figure.}
