\section*{Test 4: Figure Inside Itemize (EXPECTED FAIL)}
wrapfig2 cannot wrap figures inside list environments. When a \texttt{wrapfigure}
is placed inside \texttt{itemize}, LaTeX issues ``Stationary wrapfigure forced
to float'' warnings and pushes the figure out of the list entirely.
List items flow at full width with no wrapping.

\begin{wrapfigure}{r}{0.35\textwidth}
  \centering
  \rule{0.3\textwidth}{3cm}
  \caption{Figure inside itemize --- expected to float away.}
