%--- Test 1: Basic right wrap ---
\section*{Test 1: Basic Figure Wrapping (Right)}
Text should flow around a figure placed on the right side.
wrapstuff uses \verb|wrapstuff| environment with \verb|[r]| option.

\begin{wrapstuff}[r,width=0.35\linewidth,type=figure]
  \centering
  \rule{0.3\linewidth}{4cm}
  \caption{Right-wrapped figure.}
\end{wrapstuff}

\section*{Test 5: Centered Figure (wrapstuff-Exclusive Feature)}

with text wrapping on both sides. No other wrapfig alternative offers this.
