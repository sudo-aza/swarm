### Active Inspection (Rule 5) — Novel Angle: Parshape Leak Pattern Correlation

**Motivation:** Previous QAs counted total parshape leaks but didn't analyze the
PATTERN of leaks — which pages, how leaks propagate across pages, whether leak
width correlates with figure dimensions, and whether leaks occur on pages WITH
figures (same-page leaks) vs only on figure-less pages (cross-page leaks).

**Methodology:** Created `scripts/analyze-leak-patterns.py` (v2) with:
- Y-range-aware detection: checks if narrow text lines fall OUTSIDE the vertical
  extent of any figure on the SAME page (not just checking if the page has figures)
- Calibrated thresholds: LEFT_MARGIN_MAX=200pt, NARROW_THRESHOLD=320pt,
  FIG_ZONE_MIN_X=350pt, MIN_LINE_WIDTH=20pt
- Figure detection via `page.get_drawings()` (vector rect detection for \rule figures)
- Cross-referencing with existing `detect-parshape-leak.py` for validation

#### Same-Page Leak Findings (analyze-leak-patterns.py v2)

| Test | Same-Page Leaks | Pages Affected | Width Range |
|------|----------------|----------------|-------------|
| stress-50 | 6 | 6/13 | 287-306pt |
| customwrap | 20 | 8/10 | 133-318pt |
| pbv | 9 | 8/15 | 194-289pt |

**stress-50 detail:** 4 of 6 same-page leaks are "within fig Y" — the text line is
at the same vertical position as a figure but not covered by the figure's drawn
rectangle. This suggests the parshape exclusion zone is slightly larger than the
visible figure, OR there's a gap between adjacent stacked figures where parshape
remains narrow. 1 leak on pg3 is genuinely "below figs" — text after the last
figure's bottom remains narrowed.

**customwrap cross-page propagation detail:**
- Page 2: 1 leak, 1 page after figure (mild)
- Page 6: 3 leaks, 1 page after figure (mild)
- Page 7: 2 leaks, 2 pages after figure (new — leak spans 2 page boundaries)
- Page 8: 1 leak, 3 pages after figure (new — leak spans 3 page boundaries!)
- Page 10: 11 leaks, 1 page after figure (severe — 8 lines at 260pt width)

This confirms parshape leaks persist across MULTIPLE page boundaries (up to 3
pages observed). The existing detection script only checks the immediately
previous page for a figure source, so it may undercount the propagation distance.

**Leak width distribution insight:**
- stress-50 leaks: 287-306pt (moderate, near the ~273pt figure exclusion zone)
- customwrap leaks: 133-318pt (wide range — very narrow 133pt leaks suggest
  different figure widths create different exclusion zones that leak differently)
- pbv leaks: 194-289pt (moderate range)
