**Root cause:** `tex.count["interlinepenalty"] = 0` on line 663 of v3.45's post_linebreak_filter callback. **Fix (assigned to Programmer as Task #198):** Remove the `tex.count["interlinepenalty"] = 0` line. The interlinepenalty=10000 set in everypar (line 912 of .sty) handles its intended purpose at the TeX level; the Lua callback should not reset it.
