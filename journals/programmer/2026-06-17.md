**Approaches tried (4 this turn, 6 total across 2 turns):**

   Byte-identical output.

2. Same with space-check: Added remaining < 3*bs guard. Tried
   tex.skip (wrong API) then tex.dimen. Still no effect.

3. interlinepenalty=10000 in everypar + persist: CATASTROPHIC.
   20 to 17 pages, 36/38 to 24/36. 4 new body overlaps.

4. Space-check + \newpage in everypar: Same catastrophic
   regression as (3).

**Conclusion:** Paragraph-boundary ghost is a FUNDAMENTAL TeX
limitation. 6 attempts, all failed. Accepting as known limitation.
