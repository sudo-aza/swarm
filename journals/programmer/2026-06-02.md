### Task: #275 — Reduce near-empty pages (half-page-empty, zoe-reported)

**Context**: Zoe reported that half the page is empty after figure+wrapping. Root cause (zoe correction): "there's enough space to start, and probably end, a new paragraph, but said paragraph is wrongly deferred to the next page." The DEFER mechanism's 6bs safety margin was forcing page ejects even when there was enough space for the narrow zone.

**Approach**: Reduced DEFER safety margin from 6\baselineskip to 3\baselineskip. This allows more figures to be placed inline, reducing unnecessary page ejects. The Lua callbacks (Layer 1 pre_linebreak_filter + Layer 2 post_linebreak_filter) continue to prevent ghost narrowing for edge cases that the lower margin might miss.
